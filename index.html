A Global Movement Rooted in Love and Remembrance

In the serene Pure Land of Amitābha Buddha, we find inspiration for our journey. This celestial realm, depicted in Mahayana Buddhism, represents the ultimate destination of peace and enlightenment - a place where lotus blossoms bloom in golden ponds and celestial palaces shine with divine light.

Just as the Pure Land offers refuge to all beings, Hội Hướng seeks to create a sanctuary of compassion and wisdom in our world. Our movement draws from this vision to cultivate:

  • Boundless compassion that embraces all beings
  • Wisdom that illuminates the path to liberation
  • Pure intentions that create positive karma
  • Mindfulness that transforms every moment

The lotus flowers you see represent our spiritual journey - born in muddy waters yet rising unstained to bloom in radiant beauty. Each petal symbolizes a virtue we cultivate: compassion, wisdom, patience, diligence, generosity, morality, and meditative concentration.

"सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः सर्वे सन्तु निरामयाः।"
"May all beings be happy; may all beings be healthy."

As we build this global movement, we invite you to join us in creating a Pure Land on Earth - a world transformed by loving-kindness and awakened consciousness.

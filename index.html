A Memoir by Nguyên Thủy

The Catalyst

In loving memory of my mother, whose boundless love and unwavering spirit became the foundation of my being, I present "Nguyên Tâm - The Heart That Shaped Me".

This memoir is more than a personal journey; it's an invitation to discover the transformative power of maternal love and its capacity to shape lives across generations.

As we prepare to release this heartfelt tribute, we stand at the threshold of a global movement - a movement that seeks to honor the profound influence of those who shape our hearts and guide our paths.

The Hội Hướng Movement will awaken the world to the sacred connections that bind us, the legacies that sustain us, and the love that transcends even death itself.

The Global Hội Hướng Movement

Born from the pages of "Nguyên Tâm", the Hội Hướng Movement will inspire a worldwide awakening to the power of:

1

Sacred Remembrance

Honoring those who shaped our lives with rituals of gratitude

2

Intergenerational Healing

Bridging past and future through shared stories

3

Compassionate Legacy

Cultivating virtues that transform communities

4

Global Connection

Uniting hearts across cultures through shared humanity

"In the garden of my heart, my mother planted seeds of resilience, watered them with tears of sacrifice, and nurtured them with sunlight from her soul. Though she has returned to the earth, the blossoms of her love continue to unfold with each season of my life, perfuming the world with her eternal presence."
— Nguyên Thủy, "Nguyên Tâm - The Heart That Shaped Me"
